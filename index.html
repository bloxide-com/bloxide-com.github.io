Our Services

Bloxide is a premier electrical engineering consulting firm delivering end-to-end solutions for modern hardware challenges. We leverage the power and safety of Rust programming to create robust embedded systems that stand the test of time.

Our expertise extends beyond firmware to include high-performance cross-platform application development and comprehensive PCB design services, providing a complete technology stack for your projects.

We serve diverse markets including IoT devices, critical infrastructure, and mobility solutions, bringing reliability and innovation to every engagement.

Our Technology

The Bloxide Framework

Our open-source actor model framework brings concurrency, reliability, and modularity to embedded systems. Designed specifically for resource-constrained environments, it runs efficiently on both microcontrollers and headless Linux devices. Built with Rust's safety guarantees, it enables predictable and maintainable firmware that's easier to test and deploy.
